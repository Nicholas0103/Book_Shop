About Us

Mystery & Detective Book Shop

Our Story

Inspired by classic detective novels and Sherlock Holmes’ iconic 221B Baker Street, our bookstore was built for every fan of mystery, crime and thriller stories. We noticed local readers lacked a dedicated space for detective fiction—most general bookstores only put mystery books in a tiny corner with little attention. That’s why we created this one-stop shop fully focused on whodunits, psychological suspense and classic detective works.

What We Offer

We curate a wide collection of exclusive mystery genres: classic British detective stories, modern crime thrillers, psychological mysteries, hard-boiled detective novels and locked-room puzzle books. Every book is hand-picked to bring readers immersive, twist-filled reading experiences. We do not sell other categories like romance or fantasy to keep our store’s unique niche positioning.

Our Shop Concept

Our space adopts dark vintage tones, dim warm lighting and detective-themed decorations such as magnifying glass logos, old case files and vintage book shelves. We want readers to feel like stepping into a detective’s study, where every page turn uncovers a new secret. The website follows the same dark, warm aesthetic to mirror our physical store vibe.

Our Mission

We aim to gather all mystery lovers together, recommend hidden gem detective novels that are rarely seen in mainstream bookstores, and create a cozy reading environment for puzzle fans. Through this website, we hope more people can discover the charm of detective and mystery literature anytime, anywhere.
